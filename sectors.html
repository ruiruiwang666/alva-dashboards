Rotation Playbook
Late-Cycle Expansion with Growth Leadership

The current sector momentum picture is characteristic of a late-cycle expansion phase. Technology and Financials are leading, suggesting confidence in earnings growth and credit conditions remain supportive.

The underperformance of Real Estate, Utilities, and Energy signals that investors are rotating out of yield-sensitive and commodity-linked sectors in favor of secular growth. This pattern typically emerges when rate expectations remain elevated but the economy continues to expand.

Healthcare's strong momentum adds a quality tilt, indicating some defensive positioning alongside growth, a prudent setup for portfolios navigating late-cycle dynamics.

Recommended allocation: Tilt toward Technology, Financials, and Healthcare. Maintain neutral exposure to cyclicals. Reduce rate-sensitive sectors until the rate trajectory becomes clearer.
