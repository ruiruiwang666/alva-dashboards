🔬 Methodology

Vol-Targeted Risk Parity

Inverse-volatility weighted allocation scaled to a target portfolio volatility. Includes a drawdown circuit breaker that reduces exposure at 5% drawdown and goes to cash at 10%.

Ensemble Majority Vote

Combines short/medium/long-term momentum with SMA trend filter. Assets receive allocation only when ≥2 of 3 signals agree. Risk parity weighting with 5% cash buffer.

Parameter Optimization

Exhaustive grid search over lookback periods (5–50d), rebalance frequencies (3–7d), and volatility targets (6–20%). 288 total configurations tested across 5 asset class combinations.

Asset Universe
