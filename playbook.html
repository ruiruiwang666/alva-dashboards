Objective
The Cross-Asset Ensemble strategy aims to generate consistent, risk-adjusted returns by combining momentum signals across a diversified portfolio of crypto assets (BTC, ETH, SOL, BNB), US equities (SPY, QQQ), gold (GLD), bonds (TLT), and energy (XLE). By employing a majority-vote consensus mechanism across three independent momentum indicators and inverse-volatility (risk parity) position sizing, the strategy targets robust performance while maintaining a maximum drawdown below 5%. A permanent 5% cash buffer provides liquidity and reduces overall portfolio volatility.
Strategy Rules
Signal Generation

Three independent momentum signals are computed for each asset:

  • Short Momentum (7d): Percentage return over the last 7 trading days. Bullish if positive.
  • Medium Momentum (14d): Percentage return over the last 14 trading days. Bullish if positive.
  • Price vs SMA (30d): Current price compared to its 30-day simple moving average. Bullish if price is above SMA.
Entry Rule

An asset receives a LONG allocation only when at least 2 out of 3 signals agree (majority vote). If fewer than 2 signals are bullish, the asset allocation is set to CASH (zero weight).

Position sizing follows inverse-volatility (risk parity) weighting: each eligible asset's weight is proportional to 1 / realized_volatility, normalized so total invested weight sums to 95%.

Exit Rule

The portfolio is rebalanced every 5 trading days. At each rebalance:

  • All signals are re-evaluated for every asset.
  • Assets that no longer meet the 2/3 majority vote threshold are moved to cash.
  • Weights are re-computed using the latest realized volatility estimates.
Risk Management
  • Cash Buffer: A permanent 5% cash allocation is maintained at all times to provide liquidity and dampen volatility.
  • Inverse-Vol Weighting: Higher-volatility assets (e.g., SOL, BNB) receive smaller allocations; lower-volatility assets (e.g., GLD, TLT) receive larger allocations. This inherently limits exposure to the most volatile instruments.
  • Diversification: By spanning crypto, equities, commodities, and fixed income, the strategy benefits from cross-asset decorrelation, reducing tail risk.
  • No Leverage: The strategy is fully funded with no borrowed capital.
Parameters
  • lookback_short = 7 days
  • lookback_med = 14 days
  • lookback_long = 30 days (SMA period)
  • rebalance_freq = 5 days
  • cash_buffer = 5%
  • vote_threshold = 2 out of 3
How to Implement This Strategy
  1. Data Feed
    Set up daily data feed for 9 assets: BTC, ETH, SOL, BNB, SPY, QQQ, GLD, TLT, XLE
  2. Signal Calculation
    Calculate 3 signals daily for each asset: 7d momentum, 14d momentum, price vs 30d SMA
  3. Rebalance
    Every 5 days, rebalance portfolio — allocate to LONG assets using inverse-volatility weights
  4. Cash Buffer
    Maintain 5% cash buffer at all times to ensure liquidity and reduce portfolio volatility
  5. Risk Monitoring
    Monitor drawdown continuously — circuit breaker triggers at 10% to protect capital
